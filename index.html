Biography

I am currently a researcher at Xiao-i Research, China, working on generative artificial intelligence and large multimodal models. I received the Ph.D. degree in Information Science and Technology from the University of Tokyo, Japan, in 2022. Before entering the University of Tokyo, I received the B.E. degree in Information Engineering from Southeast University, China, in 2017, and the M.Eng. degree in Information, Production and Systems Engineering from Waseda University, Japan, in 2018.
