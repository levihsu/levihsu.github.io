Biography

I am currently a researcher at Xiao-i Research, China. I received the Ph.D. degree from Department of Creative Informatics, Graduate School of Information Science and Technology, the University of Tokyo, Japan, in 2022. Before entering the University of Tokyo, I received the B.E. degree in Information Engineering from Southeast University, China, in 2017, and the M.Eng degree from Graduate School of Information, Production and Systems, Waseda University, Japan, in 2018.
